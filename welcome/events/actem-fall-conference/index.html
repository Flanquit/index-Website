ACTEM Fall Conference

October 11, 2018 - October 12, 2018

Join Promethean at the Association of Computer Technology Educators of Maine (ACTEM) Fall Conference, October 11 – 12, 2018 at the Augusta Civic Center in Augusta, ME. The two-day conference features a combination of over 125 one-hour breakout and half-day workshops sessions. Join us to learn about Promethean’s industry-leading interactive flat panels that transform the front-of-classroom experience by providing brilliant display technology combined with a suite lesson delivery software.

For more information about the conference, visit the event page.
