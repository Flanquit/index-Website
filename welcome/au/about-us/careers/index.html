Why Promethean?

For over 20 years, Promethean has been an innovator in educational technology, creating award-winning solutions that empower teachers and inspire students to learn.

Our passion for education drives our product innovation and fuels our collaborative culture. We are a recognized, well-capitalized EdTech brand with a fun and challenging atmosphere!

Come Join Us

Benefits and Culture | We are a global company with big rewards

Find a community

We are a diverse group connected by a shared passion that fuels a culture of collaboration and care. Bring your best self, work hard, and have fun along the way.

Pursue growth

Your growth mindset contributes to the expansion of our business beyond its current horizon. We will challenge and support you to develop professionally and personally.

Make a difference

Our educational technology changes lives, and you will be integral to our success. Additionally, in the spirit of service, we offer paid time off to volunteer.

Integrate work and life

We care about your well-being. You need downtime, and life happens, so you’ll have the necessary autonomy to manage your own time while delivering results.

Our benefits packages vary from location to location, but we maintain competitive compensation and benefits throughout the globe.
