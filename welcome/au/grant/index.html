Promethean Grant 2019

Ready to enter?

Simply record a short video telling us about an inspiring story that you cherish from your teaching experience.

Whether you helped a student achieve a eureka moment or gave them the confidence to keep going, we’d love to hear about it. After all, it’s the little things that make education magical – and there’s a teacher behind every single one.

Get Started

All you need to know

The Promethean Grant will run on a termly basis, with two ActivPanels to be won in each period.

The second phrase of the competition will now run from 10th February to 9th April.

Video entries must be no longer than two minutes.

Entries must be from educators in Australia only.

Looking for Inspiration?

This year’s Promethean Grant launched at EduTECH 2019 in Sydney, where teachers shared their classroom stories on the Promethean stand. Check out a few of them!

The winner of our on-stand competition at EduTECH was Peggy Mangovski from Cessnock High School, with a brilliant story about a student with a particular interest in technology.
